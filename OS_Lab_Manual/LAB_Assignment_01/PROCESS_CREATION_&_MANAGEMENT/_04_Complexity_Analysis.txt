| Task                          | Time Complexity | Space Complexity |

| exec() commands               | **O(N)**        | **O(1)**         |
| /proc reading                 | **O(1)**        | **O(1)**         |
| CPU-intensive tasks           | **O(n)** loops  | **O(1)**         |
